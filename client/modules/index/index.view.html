Teachers & Gurus

get paid to grade

We are building a tool to make your life as a teacher easier. We believe that if instructors could spend less time grading they could spend more time teaching. We can accomplish this by making it simple to outsource your grading to subject matter experts (Gurus) around the world and within your organization or community.

How does it work?

1. Teachers submit:

Grading Rubric

A rubric with the solution(s)/answer key.

Graded Sample

An example of sample work graded properly.

Student Work

Scanned copies of the student work to be graded.

Tokens

GURU tokens for payment

2. Gurus submit:

Graded Student Work

Scanned copies of the graded work.

3. Teachers approve

4. Gurus get paid
